Vision and Mission of the Institute

Vision

To be a Multidisciplinary Technological Centre of Excellence in its myriad forms to meet the needs and demands of the futuristic society.

Mission

  • To provide the best resources and infrastructure deemed sufficient for building up a centre of excellence.
  • To nurture and foster an academic fraternity to cater to the emerging needs of the student community in particular and the society at large.
  • To encourage and motivate the students, faculties and researchers to utilize the facilities provided with their innovative and creative thinking and research.
  • To augment all available avenues to develop students in the fields of engineering, technology and allied discipline
