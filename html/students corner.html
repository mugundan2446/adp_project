National Service Scheme:

National Service Scheme was started during the year 1997. Our college is having two units of NSS team, each team have 100 NSS volunteers. The NSS units have been conducting various welfare programme and other activities in and around the city. The most recent one being Dengue fever awareness programme conducted at Veppampattu village on 28.11.13 along with the health department and village people. The Volunteers met the village people and went door to door creating awareness among the people to eradicate the mosquitoes. About 15,000 people were benefited from the awareness program.

Event Management

Event management is the process of management to the creation and development of events such as Conferences, , Seminars, Workshops and Guest Lectures etc..

Objectives
  • To complete the event within budget, schedule and meet the quality requirements of the event.
  • To work as a team and identify areas for resource sharing and optimal allocation of resources.
  • To publicize the event through media for reaching out to the society.
