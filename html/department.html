About this Department

Artificial Intelligence is the algorithmic intelligence that is possessed by the machines to perform various autonomous actions. Data Science is an interdisciplinary field with the ability to extract knowledge/insights from structured, unstructured, or semi-structured data. Twinned with Artificial Intelligence, more efficient solutions to find meaningful information from huge pools of data are possible today, with data from multiple sources – sensors, images, streaming video, satellite, medical imagery and the cloud.

Major career options

  • Business Analyst
  • Data Analyst
  • Intelligence Analyst
  • Data Manager
  • Information Security Analyst
  • Risk Analyst

Computer Science Engineering

About this Department

Vision

To emerge as a national centre of excellence for computer science with global standards and practices in providing academic excellence, research ambiance with sustained employ-ability.

Mission
  • To establish state-of-the-art infrastructure in computing for sustained progress in academics and research.
  • To provide technical support using value added training to cater to the ever changing needs of the industry and society.
  • To inculcate human values and mould them as socially responsible engineers

Program Educational Objectives
  • PEO1 : Graduates will possess sound knowledge in technical and research domains of computing to face the world at global level.
  • PEO2 : Graduates will apply the acquired knowledge to create innovative products in computing and allied areas.
  • PEO3 : Graduates will pursue higher education in world-wide renowned institutions.
  • PEO4 : Graduates will imbibe moral values and contribute for societal needs.

Electrical And Communication Engineering

About this Department

Vision

The Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering of Sriram Engineering College aims to be a Centre of Excellence in its domain, which provides enterprising solutions for societal needs.

Mission
  • To produce self-learning, self-motivated, responsible professionals equipped to meet the challenges in the day to day life through a quality and disciplined learning environment with committed staff.
  • To impart sound technical proficiency and quality education for students to augment the employability and ethical values.
  • To provide beneficial environment for students with excellent amenities to improve research and developmental activities.

Program Educational Objectives:

  • Our graduates shall exhibit professional attitude, good leadership and interpersonal skills.
  • Our Graduates shall contribute to the growth of the nation and society by applying acquired intelligence in all technical, computing and managerial areas.
  • Our Graduates shall be a successful member (or) a leader in diverse teams with enhanced administrative skills and financial management.
  • Our Graduates shall propagate the need for lifelong learning and adapt themselves to recent trends.
