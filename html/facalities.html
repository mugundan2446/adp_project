AUDITORIUM

The college has an air conditioned indoor auditorium acoustically treated with a seating capacity of 1200 audience. This auditorium has an immense pride of having hosted several National / International Level Symposia and Conferences. There is also an air conditioned mini-auditorium with a pleasing ambiance and a seating capacity of about 300 participants to host smaller events.

LIBRARY

Sriram Engineering College Central Library is the soul of the institution and it acquires, processes, preserves and disseminates information to the user community with the motto of empowering knowledge. It is established in 1993. It is located next to the main auditorium building in a vast area of 650(Sq M). The Library and Information Centre consists of a Reference Section, Circulation Section, Periodical Section, New Arrival Book Section, Book Bank Section, Question Bank Section and the Digital Library. The Library is well protected with fire alarms & CCTV security system.In this fully automated Library, students and staff members can search the books by Author, Title, Acc No, Publisher or subject heading and their availability. Each student is given 4 Library books at a time and four books are given to each staff. Library hour is scheduled for students every week in their Time Table.The Central Library of Sriram Engineering College is a registered institutional member of DELNET, NDLI and NPTEL.

Special Features of Central Library

  • At the Central Library, we use the E-gate entry: it serves the Library users when users approach the e- gate, their identities are verified through scanner methods, and their relevant information is displayed on the computer.
  • Librarians ensure the best possible information services.
  • Standard texbooks and books by well-known authors are stocked and every effort is made to obtain all titles recommended by the faculty members.
  • Multiple copies of popular books are bought to make them available to as many students as possible.
  • All students and faculty of the college are eligible for the membership of the Library.
  • The Autolib software is used for Library automation.
  • All the books are bar coded in the Library& barcode laser scanners are used in the circulation counter for book transactions.
  • The Library area is under CCTV surveillance.

100 Mbps Internet

Our College has 100 Mbps Internet connection (leased Line) locally networked and connected through Fiber Optic Cables,(point to point).The central Library servers facilitate the academic requirement of students and staff. It also provides access to e-books, study material, previous university question papers etc. through the college Local Area Network. A well established internet lab provides a high speed internet connection to students for browsing and knowledge development.

Hostel

The college is having fully furnished separate hostels for boys and girls. The Boys Hostel can accommodate 250 students and the Girls Hostel, 150 students. Both hostels are facilitated with well-equipped Gyms to provide the students an opportunity to keep themselves physically fit. A common mess complex with a Bio-gas Plant for cooking purposes and a plinth area of 3800 sqm is available to accommodate dining halls for boys, girls, staff and guests separately. Government Certified visiting doctor Dr. B. Srinivasan ensures prompt solutions to health problems of the inmates. 24hrs Reverse Osmosis plant water supply to the hostel, mess and the entire campus.
